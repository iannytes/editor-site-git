About Me

A seasoned writer who takes your content seriously

My name is Ian Nytes. I'm a developmental editor and content consultant with a background in web content and fiction writing. I grew up in the beating heartland of the American Midwest and developed a passion for writing early in life. I've written a total of 3 novels and have countless hours of web development experience, which means I understand the creative process from both sides of the desk. I know how hard it is to write something you care about and how vulnerable it can feel to share that work with others. That's why I approach every project with empathy, honesty, and a commitment to helping you make your story the best it can be. Professionally, I've recently won an award from Down Avenue for branching narritive and have a novel currently on submission. If you'd like to check out my personal projects feel free to navigate over to the projects page!

I specialize in science fiction, fantasy and romance but I have a deep love for all genres of fiction and a wide range of experience with different storytelling styles. Whether you're writing an epic fantasy web serial, a character-driven literary novel, or a fast-paced thriller, I can help you identify the strengths of your story and the areas that need work. My goal is to give you the tools and insights you need to take your writing to the next level.

I'm also a big reader and my taste can very wildly. I love stories that are emotionally resonant, thematically rich, and structurally inventive. Check out my GoodReads profile here to see what I'm reading and what I love.

What I Do

Big-picture feedback that actually moves the needle and the small details that make all the difference.

Whether it's a manuscript or web content, most drafts have invisible problems that cost you readers — or conversions. Stories lose momentum through pacing issues and weak character motivation. Website copy fails to connect with your audience or rank in search because the messaging isn't clear.

I diagnose these problems and solve them. For manuscripts, I give you a real map for revision at the structural level. For web copy, I optimize for both human readers and search engines — making sure your message lands, your SEO resonates, and your content converts.

You'll walk away with concrete, actionable feedback designed to move your project forward — whether that's a reader's journey through your story or a visitor's path to taking action on your site.
